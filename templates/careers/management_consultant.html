Management Consultant

Advise organizations on strategy, operations, and performance improvement to achieve business goals.

Test Your Fit

Job Description

Analyze business problems, recommend solutions, and assist organizations in implementing improvements for efficiency and growth.

Job Outlook

Growth: +11% by 2034

New Jobs: 15,000+

Automation Risk: Medium

Salary (U.S.)

Bottom 10%: $64,000

Median: $93,000

Top 10%: $208,000+

Duties

  • 📊 Analyze business operations and data
  • 💡 Recommend strategic and operational improvements
  • 🤝 Work with clients to implement changes
  • 📈 Evaluate outcomes and optimize processes
  • ⚡ Prepare reports and presentations for stakeholders

Skills & Qualities

  • 🧠 Analytical and critical thinking
  • 📊 Business and financial acumen
  • 💬 Communication and presentation
  • ⚡ Problem-solving and adaptability
  • 🤝 Collaboration with clients and teams

Working Conditions

  • 💻 Office, client sites, and travel
  • 🕒 Standard hours, may include travel or long days
  • 🤝 Work closely with clients and colleagues
  • 📊 Frequent reports and presentations
  • 📚 Continuous learning of industry trends

Career Progression

Entry: Analyst / Associate Consultant
Mid-Level: Consultant / Senior Consultant
Senior: Principal / Engagement Manager
Leadership: Partner / Director

Qualifications & Training

  • 🎓 Bachelor’s or Master’s in Business, Finance, or related field
  • 💻 Experience in consulting, finance, or business analytics
  • 📘 Optional: MBA or certifications (PMP, Lean Six Sigma)
  • 💡 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
