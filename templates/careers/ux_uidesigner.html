UX/UI Designer

Create engaging and user-friendly digital experiences by designing interfaces, improving usability, and enhancing user satisfaction.

Test Your Fit

Job Description

Design user interfaces, conduct usability testing, create wireframes/prototypes, and collaborate with developers to implement designs.

Job Outlook

Growth: +18% by 2033

New Jobs: 22,000+

Automation Risk: Low

Salary (U.S.)

Bottom 10%: $55,000

Median: $95,000

Top 10%: $150,000+

Duties

  • 🎨 Design UI/UX for apps & websites
  • 📝 Create wireframes, mockups, & prototypes
  • 🔍 Conduct usability tests & gather feedback
  • 🤝 Collaborate with developers & stakeholders
  • 📈 Improve overall user experience

Skills & Qualities

  • 🎨 Visual design & creativity
  • 🧠 UX research & problem-solving
  • 💻 Prototyping & design tools (Figma, Adobe XD)
  • 📝 Communication & collaboration
  • 🌱 Curiosity & empathy for users

Working Conditions

  • 💻 Office or remote work
  • 🕒 40h/week, flexible schedule
  • 🤝 Team collaboration with developers & product managers
  • ⚡ Fast-paced project deadlines
  • 📚 Continuous learning of design trends

Career Progression

Entry: Junior UX/UI Designer
Mid-Level: UX/UI Designer
Senior: Senior Designer / UX Lead
Leadership: Design Manager / Creative Director

Qualifications & Training

  • 🎓 Bachelor’s in Design, HCI, or related field
  • 📘 Optional certifications in UX/UI tools & research
  • 🕒 3–4 years degree + portfolio of design projects
  • 💡 Continuous learning of design trends & usability best practices
