Obstetrician-Gynecologist (OB-GYN)

Care for women’s reproductive health and childbirth.

Test Your Fit

Job Description

Provide medical care to women, including pregnancy, childbirth, and reproductive system issues.

Job Outlook

Growth: +7% by 2034

New Jobs: 2,500+

Automation Risk: Low

Salary (U.S.)

Bottom 10%: $150,000

Median: $300,000

Top 10%: $450,000+

Duties

  • 🤰 Provide prenatal and postnatal care
  • 🩺 Perform gynecological exams
  • 📝 Document patient history
  • 💊 Prescribe medications and treatments
  • 👩‍⚕️ Conduct deliveries and surgeries

Skills & Qualities

  • 🧠 Knowledge of obstetrics and gynecology
  • 🤝 Compassion and patient care
  • ⚡ Problem-solving and decision-making
  • 💡 Attention to detail and precision
  • 📊 Analytical and diagnostic skills

Working Conditions

  • 🏥 Hospitals, clinics, private practice
  • 🕒 40–60 hrs/week, may include on-call shifts
  • 💻 Use medical equipment and electronic records
  • 📚 Ongoing training and certification
  • 🤝 Collaborate with nurses and staff

Career Progression

Entry: OB-GYN Resident
Mid-Level: Staff OB-GYN
Senior: Senior OB-GYN
Leadership: Chief OB-GYN

Qualifications & Training

  • 🎓 Medical degree (MD/DO)
  • 📘 OB-GYN residency
  • 💻 Board certification
  • 📚 Continuing education in women’s health
