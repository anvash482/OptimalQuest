Graphic Designer

Create visual content to communicate ideas and captivate audiences.

Test Your Fit

Job Description

Design graphics for print, digital, and social media, translating concepts into compelling visuals.

Job Outlook

Growth: +3% by 2033

New Jobs: 8,000+

Automation Risk: Medium

Salary (U.S.)

Bottom 10%: $38,000

Median: $54,680

Top 10%: $93,000+

Duties

  • 🎨 Create graphics for digital & print
  • 💡 Collaborate on visual concepts
  • 📝 Present ideas to clients & teams
  • 📊 Maintain brand consistency
  • ⚡ Update designs based on feedback

Skills & Qualities

  • 🖌 Creativity & visual storytelling
  • 💻 Adobe Creative Suite & Figma
  • 🎨 Typography & color theory
  • 📝 Communication & collaboration
  • ⚡ Attention to detail

Working Conditions

  • 💻 Office or remote work
  • 🕒 40h/week typical
  • 🤝 Team projects & client collaboration
  • 📊 Deadlines for campaigns & projects
  • 📚 Continuous learning of new tools & trends

Career Progression

Entry: Junior Graphic Designer
Mid-Level: Graphic Designer
Senior: Senior Designer / Art Lead
Leadership: Art Director

Qualifications & Training

  • 🎓 Bachelor’s in Graphic Design, Visual Arts, or related
  • 💻 Portfolio of creative projects
  • 📘 Optional: Certification in design tools
  • 💡 Internship experience preferred
